# constructive feedback

## Core idea
I compartmentalize personal notions about the way something should be and allow people to solve problems their way, respecting the work they put in it. I focus on giving feedback according to objective metrics and give reasons for my recommendations of improvement. I make sure to keep my constructive criticism within my area of expertise and not nit pick. I never try to make people feel shame. I always look for opportunities to praise what people do well.
