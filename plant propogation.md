# plant propogation

## Core idea
Cuttings: Snipping a piece of a healthy stem or leaf and placing it in water or moist soil until it grows roots.
Division: Digging up and splitting a clumping plant and its root system into smaller, separate sections.
Layering: Bending a low-lying stem down to the ground and covering it with soil so it forms roots while still attached to the parent plant.
Grafting: Joining a stem or bud from one plant onto the root system of another plant so they grow as one
