# product marketing

## Core idea
Product marketing is the process of bringing a product to market and driving its ongoing adoption. It serves as the strategic bridge between a company’s product, sales, and marketing teams. Its core focus is on understanding the target audience, crafting the product's narrative, and ensuring it solves real customer problems.
Product marketing managers (PMMs) typically handle the following tasks:
Positioning & Messaging: Defining who the product is for and translating complex technical features into simple, compelling stories that resonate with buyers.
Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy: Mapping out how, when, and where a new product or feature will be launched to maximize awareness and adoption.
Sales Enablement: Equipping the sales team with the resources they need to close deals, such as competitor battlecards, pitch decks, and product demos.
Market Research: Gathering customer feedback and analyzing competitor data to figure out exactly what the market needs.
