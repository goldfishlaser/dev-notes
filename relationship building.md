# relationship building

## Core idea
1) Listen actively- In my experience, people who feel heard will speak to you. If something doesn't make sense to me, I ask for clarification. If I feel I might understand, I repeat it back to make sure.  
2) Have genuine interest - I think that everyone's role is interesting and will teach me something. I find opportunities to ask people questions and give them an opportunity to share. 
3) Am reliable - I make myself available to people when they need me. I try to avoid cancelling meetings and communicate ahead of time if I need to reschedule.  
4) Am open minded - I don't try to force my way on others. I learn about other cultures to avoid offense.
