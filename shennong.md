# shennong

## Core idea
Shennong (神農), known as the "Divine Farmer" or "Divine Husbandman". A legendary ruler and culture hero, he is credited with inventing the plow, discovering the [[Five Grains]] and establishing the foundation of [[traditional Chinese medicine]] and [[herbalism]].
 Shennong is credited with introducing the [[hoe]], [[axe]], digging [[wells]], [[irrigation]], and the [[farmers market]].
