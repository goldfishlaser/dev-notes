# windbreaks

## Core idea

Plants or structures placed to prevent wind damage to more sensitive plants. 

CV homestead will require windbreaks for strong westerly and southwesterly wind.

Tree options:
Overstory (Tall Canopy): 
Arizona Cypress (Cupressus arizonica) 
Rocky Mountain Juniper (Juniperus scopulorum). 
These remain evergreen to block winter winds.

Understory (Dense Mid-Layer): 
Four-wing Saltbush (Atriplex canescens) 
Desert Willow (Chilopsis linearis). 
These catch lower-level gusts.

Nitrogen-Fixing Support: 
New Mexico Locust (Robinia neomexicana) 
Adds fertility while slowing the wind.
